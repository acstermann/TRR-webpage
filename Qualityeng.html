Quality & Environment

Through the combination of different technics and modern machines TRR Tankreinigung GmbH is ensuring that we use our cleaning products effective. This results in excellent wastewater and exhaust treatments.

  • Effective cleaning
    high pressure technic, different attachments and individual cleaning programs are the guarantee for a thorough and careful cleaning.

  • Gentle wastewater treatment and preparation
    Accumulating wastewater is collected and reprocessed in the wastewater and filtration plants in order to be able to clean in the most environmentally friendly way possible.

  • Separate food lane
    Separate clothes and absolute spatial separation allow strict separation of chemicals and food in our cleaning.

Quality standards, safety as well as health and environmental protection are consistently implemented at TRR Tankreinigung GmbH.

Behavior-based safety and quality are promoted through recurring and ongoing training and education and are an expression of the understanding of quality within the company philosophy.

To maintain the level of quality and safety, technical check systems are also used that go far beyond the minimum legal requirements.

TRR Tankreinigung GmbH is a member of the German Association for Tank Interior Cleaning (DVTI) and is regularly certified.
